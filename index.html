The EU AI Act Protects Creators

The EU AI Act (2024) establishes the world's first comprehensive legal framework for artificial intelligence. For illustrators, it is the most significant legal protection in the history of digital art.

SIP translates complex legal language into tools you can actually use — because understanding your rights shouldn't require a law degree.

Article 53

Opt-Out from Training Data

Creators may formally request exclusion of their work from AI training datasets. SIP automates this process.

Article 13

Transparency Requirements

AI systems must disclose when and how creative works were used. SIP logs and tracks these disclosures.

Article 86

Right to Explanation

You have the right to understand how AI decisions affecting your work were made. SIP surfaces these explanations.
